Planting Day Notes — May 4, 2026

  • Water plants thoroughly the evening before planting, while still in their trays.
  • Dig holes twice as wide as the plug and the same depth. Don't bury the crown.
  • Spacing: 18–24" between plants for all four species. They'll fill in by year 2.
  • Water in deeply at planting and again 2–3× per week for the first 3 weeks if no rain.
  • Extras from the Flex pile should go into the Septic Lawn by default — there's nothing planted there yet, so it can absorb as much as we have.
  • Plant tags: save at least one tag per species in case we need to reference supplier info later.
